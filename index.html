About Me

I'm a Robotics Engineer and Research Assistant with an MSc in Robotics from Cranfield University. My expertise lies in developing autonomous robotic systems with multi-sensor perception and intelligent decision-making capabilities.

Currently, I'm contributing to the MANNA project at Cranfield University, developing multi-sensor navigation algorithms for autonomous maritime platforms operating in GNSS-denied environments using astronomical sensing and IMU fusion.

My research interests focus on field robotics, robust autonomy in challenging environments, and emerging approaches such as Vision-Language-Action models and embodied AI for intelligent robotic behavior.
